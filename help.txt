Game contains all the features required in the assignment including some bonuses.
How to play the Game.
left,right,up arrow keys to move left,right,up.
'A' to throw water Baloons.
water baloon can kill Fire Beams but not FireLines(slanting Lines).
Player has 3 lives Initially, If he uses all of them ,game gets over and window will quit.
Shield and Extra Life are Projectile Bonuses player may get.
Player's score would be incremented on coin Collection.There are two types of Coins. Coin2 has double value than Coin1.
